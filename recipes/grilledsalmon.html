Grilled Salmon

Description

Follow these easy steps for the best grilled salmon recipe that's about to become your new favorite healthy summertime dinner.

Ingrediants

  • A nice fillet of salmon, king, chinook, coho, or sockeye. Choose a salmon fillet with the skin ON.
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Apply non-stick spray to a piece of foil.
  2. Put salmon on foil skin side down.
  3. Brush salmon with olive oil.
  4. Lightly season with sea salt, black pepper and squeeze of lemon
  5. Close foil over salmon
  6. Grill on lower rack over direct heat for 12-15 minutes
  7. Grill until internal temperature is 145F.
  8. Remove from foil, flip over and grill for one minute to sear.
