Grilled Chicken

Description

Say goodbye to dry, bland grilled chicken breasts. This recipe guarantess juicy, flavorful chicken every time.

Ingrediants

  • Nice boneless chicken breast fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Coat both sides of chicken with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, pepper or desired seasonings.
  2. Preheat gas grill before cooking to medium-high heat, or 350-400F.
  3. Lightly grease grill surface and add chicken.
  4. Close lid and cook 8 minutes, or until bottoms are browned with sear marks.
  5. Rotate chicken, move chicken to upper rack, close lid, reduce heat to medium, and cook 7-10 minutes more, or until internal temperature reaches 165F.
  6. Remove chicken breast from the grill and let rest 5 minutes before serving.
